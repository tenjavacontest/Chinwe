Chinwe
============
I am doing the 08:00 contest, based on Ian Fleming's James Bond books :)


Commands:
  /bond - Toggle employment - permission: bond.bond
  /bondadmin - Admin commands - permission: bond.bondadmin

Plugin information:
  When you become an Agent, you are given 3 gadgets, a tuxedo and a martini.
  Mouse over these items to view how to use them

Extra permission nodes:

bond.laser: Allow usage of the laser gadget
bond.grapple: Allow usage of the grapple watch
bond.pen: Allow usage of the explosive pen
bond.gunmurder: Allow the killing of another player with the gun

Keep an eye on your experience bar and level, they show your ammo/bomb fuse!
Try scrolling through your hotbar, your xp bar/level updates depending on what you're holding :>
